About Me

I’m Egemen Erken, a Web GIS student passionate about technology, maps, and data visualization. I enjoy building web-based tools that connect geography and design. In my free time, I explore new web technologies, create simple applications, and experiment with OpenLayers and JavaScript.

My long-term goal is to work in GIS development, combining cartography, programming, and creative design.
